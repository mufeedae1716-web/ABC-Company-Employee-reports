# ABC Company - Employee Data Analysis

## Project Overview
This project involves a comprehensive analysis of an employee dataset from ABC Company, consisting of 458 rows and 9 columns. The objective is to preprocess the data, perform various statistical analyses, and present the findings through effective graphical representations.

## Project Tasks

### 1. Data Preprocessing
- **Height Correction:** The original 'height' column was replaced with random numbers between 150 and 180 to ensure data consistency and integrity for the analysis.

### 2. Analysis Tasks
- **Team Distribution:** Calculated the number of employees in each team and their respective percentage split relative to the total workforce.
- **Position Segregation:** Categorized employees based on their specific roles/positions within the company.
- **Age Group Analysis:** Identified the most predominant age group among the employees.
- **Salary Expenditure:** Discovered which team and position account for the highest total salary spending.
- **Age vs. Salary Correlation:** Investigated the relationship between an employee's age and their salary.

### 3. Graphical Representation
The following visualizations were created to represent the data:
- Bar Charts for Team Distribution and Position Segregation.
- Histograms/Pie Charts for Age Group identification.
- Heatmaps or Bar Charts for Salary Expenditure by Team/Position.
- Scatter Plots to visualize the correlation between Age and Salary.
